About Conference

Welcome to TechConX, the premier technology conference of the year. Join us for a two-day event filled with cutting-edge sessions, workshops, and inspiring keynotes. This year's theme is "Innovation Unleashed," exploring the latest trends and advancements in the tech world. Save the date: June 15-16, 2024 at TechHub Convention Center. Don't miss out on the opportunity to hear from our esteemed keynote speakers, including Dr. Tech Guru and Prof. Coding Master.
